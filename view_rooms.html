Property Description

Opened: 1976

Renovated: 2004

Number of Rooms: 506

The hotel is a large business hotel, which won the "China Hotel Golden Star Award" in 2010, the honor of China's hotel industry. The hotel stands on Huanshi East Road in the central business district of Guangzhou and the core of the golden business circle. Stylish and modern room design; well-equipped conference and banquet venues; various dining options; considerate service to create perfect moments for you.

The hotel is surrounded by famous international famous stores and high-end office buildings, showing the style of international famous cities: La Perle Plaza, World Trade Center, Friendship Store, which are colorful and exciting; it is adjacent to the famous style bar street, which not only gathers famous flavors of Chaozhou, Guangdong, Hunan, Sichuan, etc. Food, more concentrated classic food from all over the world, guests can enjoy the prosperity and charm of modern metropolis within five minutes' walk.

Hotel transportation is convenient, cost-effective. The subway station is close at hand, and it only takes a short drive to the exhibition center. It is an ideal choice for Guangzhou exhibition hotel reservations such as the Canton Fair.

The rooms are equipped with minibars, personal safes, etc., and the public facilities are equipped with gymnasiums and swimming pools. The high-altitude swimming pool located on the 33rd floor allows you to swim in the sky and have a panoramic view of the city.

The hotel also has Baiyunxuan Chinese Restaurant, Baiyunyan Chinese Restaurant, Xiangyun Western Restaurant, and Italian Restaurant, presenting you authentic Cantonese cuisine, exquisite refreshments, or Western cuisine.

The hotel is equipped with large multi-functional halls and meeting rooms of different specifications, which can meet the choices of various business meetings and banquets.
